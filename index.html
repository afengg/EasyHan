About EasyHan

EasyHan is a Korean vocabularly practice tool that serves as a handy tool for students of the language to memorize Korean words, which is a crucial part of any language. Although students may feel passionate about learning Korean, it can be hard in practice to get students motivated to actually self-learn on their own time. EasyHan gives them a clear track to progress on, so students do not have to plan their own course, which can often kill the desire to learn.

With multimedia support, each vocabulary word is not just presented by itself. Graphics and recorded audio accompany them to provide a plethora of information to effectively learn not just how to read, but also how to speak and type.

EasyHan also intelligently adjusts to students based on their performance on individual vocabulary words. It employs a spaced repetition system that increases the frequency of appearance for words that seem problematic for the user, and decreases the frequency for words that seem to be committed to memory. This helps the student make the most of their time on the program.
